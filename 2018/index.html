#AcidDecember Overdrive

Greetings 2018 Acid People!

For thousands of years an intergalactic tradition happens occasionally on very special winters. What makes those winter special is that Acid December takes place IN OUR DIMENSION!

It was originally initiated by the amazing people at DevSound while they were inventing Lemon Trees. Incidentally, a seed fell into the internet and roots spread all over the globe calling contributors to compulsively send acid tracks to be released one by one during December and beyond, depending on the amount of summoned contributors.
